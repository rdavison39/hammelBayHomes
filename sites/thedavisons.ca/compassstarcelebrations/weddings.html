Types of Wedding Ceremonies

I celebrate and support any two people who are in love and want to get married.

Wedding
Ceremonies

If you have found “the one” and want to celebrate with a splash, I can help!

We will get to know each other on Zoom or in person, and with your input I will write a ceremony which reflects and celebrates your love story.

Whether you want your wedding to be quiet and intimate, raucous and fun, serious and refined, or any combination of these, your ceremony will be significant and meaningful.

Elopement
Ceremonies

Perhaps you just want to whisk away to your favourite place, or stay in the comfort of your home to say “I do” to the one you love.

I can help by putting together a ceremony that reflects and celebrates your love, commitment and dedication to each other.

Simple
Ceremonies

You may just want a simple legal ceremony to recognize and celebrate your love for, and commitment to, the person you want to have by your side in life.

Or, perhaps you are having a destination wedding and need to be legally married before you go.

Either way, I can help you mark the moment with significance.

I love to travel and am happy to join you for your ceremony in different areas of the province, including Halton, Hamilton, Niagara, Kitchener-Waterloo, Toronto, Collingwood, Muskoka, Huntsville, North Bay, eastern Ontario and everywhere in between.
